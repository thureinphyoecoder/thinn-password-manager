Thinn Password Manager

This app exists for one reason.

To let you keep your passwords without involving anyone else.

Passwords should belong to you.
Not to servers. Not to companies. Not to subscriptions.

Thinn is a local-only password manager. Everything stays on your machine. There is no account system, no sync service, and no background connection to the internet.

If something feels boring here, that is probably intentional.

No cloud.
No lock-in.
Just your vault, on your machine.
